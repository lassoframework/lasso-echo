# Brand Voice Intake (EXAMPLE template - copy, fill with the client, keep the numbered headings)

The numbered `## N.` headings are the anchors `python -m agent draft-bible` reads.
Write in the client's own words; the drafter never invents content, and any section
you leave empty becomes an explicit TODO in the draft bible.

## 1. Who you are
The business in the owner's own words: what you do, who for, what makes you
different, your story in three sentences.

## 2. Who you talk to
Your best member, described like a person: age range, life situation, what they
struggle with, what result they want, what almost stopped them from joining.

## 3. Voice and tone
Words and phrases you actually say. Words you would never say. Formal or casual.
Emoji or none. Anything you refuse to sound like.

## 4. Hard guardrails
Anything Echo must NEVER say or show: prices you do not want public, competitors,
topics, member names without permission, promotions that ended.

## 5. Content pillars
Three to five topics you want to be known for, and for each: one hook line and one
or two body lines in your own words.

## 6. Social proof
Real quotes and numbers, one block per entry, blank line between entries:

Quote: The exact words the member said.
Attribution: First name L., member since 2024
Permission: yes
Verified: 2026-07-02

Stat: 18 pounds down in 12 weeks
Support: First strength block
Attribution: Mike R.
Permission: yes
Verified: 2026-07-02

Entries without "Permission: yes" are listed as skipped and never used.

## 7. CTAs, links, and hashtags
The one link you want people to tap, three to five call-to-action lines in your
voice, and any hashtags you already use.
