## Who LASSO serves
Boutique fitness: CrossFit affiliates, strength, HIIT, functional fitness
microgyms, boutique studios. Best fit: gyms already doing about $15,000+ per
month. Not for discount gyms or free trial models. Suggested advertised
membership minimum about $200 per month.

## Frameworks (safe to reference)
- Funnel diagnostic order: close rate, then show rate, then booking behavior,
  then lead volume. "We truly don't care about the number of leads. We care
  about sign ups."
- Growth scorecard: leads, appointments booked, appointments showed, new
  clients, new monthly revenue, lost clients.
- The Halo Effect: paid ads drive growth beyond directly attributed leads.
- Speed to lead standard: first response within 5 minutes, first call within
  15 minutes, 10 to 20 touchpoints over about 36 days.
- Marketing funnel stages: awareness, consideration, evaluation, commitment.
- Growth stages: Startup Grind (0 to 15k), Tinker (15 to 30k), Leadership
  Development (30 to 60k).
- BANNED until Blake confirms they are real LASSO terms: "4 Leg Marketing
  Stool," "3 Step Plan," "Booked to Close Calculator."

## Hard guardrail (unchanged)
LASSO is a lead gen company. Never imply gyms do not need more leads. The old
"you don't have a lead problem" line is BANNED for the LASSO brand. Instead:
"Most gyms don't have a lead problem; they have a follow up problem" is the
approved framing (leads still matter, follow up multiplies them).
