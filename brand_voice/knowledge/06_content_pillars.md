# CONTENT PILLARS AND HOOKS (approved source)

## The seven pillars
1. PAID MARKETING IS NON NEGOTIABLE. If you are not marketing, you are dying.
   Organic caps you around 100-150 members. Franchises outspend you. The Halo
   Effect means ads lift everything.
2. FOLLOW UP AND SPEED TO LEAD. Respond in 5 minutes. The fortune is in the
   follow up. 10-20 touchpoints. A human touch beats pure automation.
3. SALES AS COACHING. Selling is coaching. Kill free trials, run consultations.
   The 70%+ close rate standard.
4. KNOW YOUR NUMBERS. Churn 3-5%. Care about sign ups, not lead counts.
   Profitability over cost per lead.
5. CLEAR MESSAGING. The client is the hero; the gym is the guide. One liner.
   Sell outcomes, not workouts.
6. RETENTION AND OPERATIONS. Onboarding 4-8 sessions. Reach out at day 8 of a
   no show. Hire a coach at 75-100 members.
7. STAGE BASED GROWTH. Startup Grind, Tinker, Leadership Development. Advice
   must match your stage. Pinnacle for $30k-$100k operators.
