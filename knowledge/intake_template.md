# CLIENT INTAKE (Stage 3 onboarding template)

Copy this file, fill it WITH the client, keep the numbered `## N.` headings:
they are the anchors the onboarding pipeline and draft-bible read. Every
section below is REQUIRED; an empty or TODO section blocks the scaffold with
the missing list, nothing is ever guessed. Structure mirrors the brand bible.

## 1. Who you are (business + offers)
The business in the owner's own words: what you do, who for, what makes you
different. List the offers you want mentioned publicly, in exact wording.
Anything not written here never appears in a post.

## 2. Who you talk to (avatar)
Your best member described like a person: age range, life situation, what they
struggle with, what result they want.

## 3. Voice and tone
Words and phrases you actually say. Words you would never say. Formal or
casual. Emoji or none.

## 4. Hard guardrails and locked claims
Anything Echo must NEVER say or show: prices you do not want public,
competitors, topics, member names without permission. List any numbers or
claims that are PENDING verification; they stay locked until you confirm them
in writing.

## 5. Content pillars
Three to five topics you want to be known for, each with one hook line and one
or two body lines in your own words.

## 6. Social proof
Real quotes and numbers, one block per entry (Quote or Stat, Attribution,
Permission: yes, Verified: YYYY-MM-DD). Entries without permission are never
used.

## 7. CTAs, links, and hashtags
The one link you want people to tap, three to five call to action lines in
your voice, and hashtags you already use.

## 8. Posting preferences
Days and times you want posts, days to skip, platforms (Instagram, Facebook
Page), and anything seasonal.

## 9. Consent policy
Your rule for photos of people: who may appear, how consent is recorded, and
who at the gym confirms it. Assets with people and no recorded consent are
excluded automatically when the consent guard is armed.
