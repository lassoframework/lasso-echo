## DAY 5 (Angle 4) THE HALO EFFECT
Card: headline "Your best leads never click the ad."

Caption:
They see your ad. They do not click. They look you up that night. They scroll your page. Two weeks later they walk in.

Your dashboard calls that organic. It is not.

In our book we tell the story of Matt at CrossFit Newtown. His ads showed 10 direct joins and the math felt thin. Then he asked his walk ins how they found him. Twenty two more members that period had seen the ads. His real cost per member fell from 150 dollars to under 50.

We call it the Halo Effect. There is a whole chapter on it.

## DAY 7 (Angle 5) CASE STUDY: PAT
Card: SPLIT archetype in cover style. Left "Everyone 18 to 65." Right "Three real avatars." Bottom line "20 percent to 60 percent."

Caption:
Pat ran CrossFit Loup for years. Great gym. His targeting was everyone aged 18 to 65 within 15 miles who likes fitness.

That is not a strategy. That is a prayer.

One in five consults joined. Four out of five walked back out.

We rebuilt his targeting around three real people. Former athletes getting back in shape. Busy professionals who wanted accountability. Parents looking for a community to commit to. Each got its own creative and its own language.

Ninety days later his conversion rate tripled. Twenty percent to sixty.

Same gym. Same coaches. Same price. The people walking in already saw themselves in the ad.

Full story in our book The Full Gym.
