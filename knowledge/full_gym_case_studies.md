# THE FULL GYM — Case Studies (Approved Source)
All nineteen case studies below appear by name in our book The Full Gym. We put these gyms in print ourselves, which is why Echo may reference them in social copy WITH our book named as the source. These are our clients and our results; the copy should carry that pride. Numbers must match this file exactly. If a number is not here, it does not exist.

STANDING RULE: posts that name a client gym cite our book ("from our book The Full Gym") so the citation is our published work, not a private client record. First person voice, always.

1. Mark and Ben, CrossFit Future. 120 members, flat for six months. After restructured targeting, persona landing pages, a standardized consultation framework, and follow up cadence: 106 new members in three months, 197 members by month four, second location by year two.
2. Aimee, Swift River CrossFit. 20 to 40 inquiries a month but an 18 percent conversion rate. Fix: structured consultation, objection scripts, three touch follow up. Result: 13,000 dollars in new monthly recurring revenue within 12 weeks, no change to ad spend.
3. Lauren and Christina, Fit Mamas Tribe. Strong avatar, stuck under 20K MRR because the sale was transactional. Connection Clarity Confidence consultation. Consultation to membership jumped from 55 to 81 percent. 30,000 MRR by month three, 31,500 by month five at a 78 percent rate.
4. Mikilah, FemFit Redding. Copy shifted from feature dumps to identity: "You used to feel strong. Somewhere along the way, that got lost." Click through and lead quality rose; prospects arrived emotionally invested.
5. Shayla, North Naples CrossFit. Burned budget in 4 days on pure conversion objective. The 30 40 30 split: reached 12,000 people, 400 landing page visits, 25 high quality leads per month, cost per member down 40 percent.
6. Jeff, Friendship Fitness. 100 leads a month, closing 18. Added hybrid capture plus a 48 hour cadence (email in 5 minutes, text in 2 hours, call in 24, second text at 48). 18 to 24 members per month, a 33 percent lift, same spend.
7. Pat, CrossFit Loup. Targeting was "everyone 18 to 65 who likes fitness," converting 20 percent. Three avatar specific audiences with their own creative. Conversion tripled to 60 percent in 90 days.
8. Matt, CrossFit Newtown. 1,500 a month in ads looked thin at 10 direct joins, but 22 more members that period had seen the ads and joined through referral or walk in. Real cost per member fell from 150 to under 50. The Halo Effect chapter is built on this.
9. Ibrahim, VIBE Boxing Fitness. 300 a month and panic toggling got him stuck at 5,000 monthly revenue. Committed to 1,500 a month, the 14 day window, and 20 percent per week scaling. 18 members month one, 36 total by month two, revenue past 12,000 in 60 days at 470 per member.
10. Shaun, Hoosier Athletic Club, Bloomington Indiana. Fourteen years, zero paid ads, hit the organic ceiling. LASSO system: 33 new members in 60 days, 18 in a single month, over 3,500 in new monthly revenue.
11. Tommy, District H Strength. 50 to 60 leads a month, 12 converting. Rebuilt the handoff: personal text in 2 hours, confirmation sequence, standardized consultation, no show rescue. Show rate 40 to 68 percent, close rate 35 to 55 percent, same spend.
12. Dave, Courage Fitness Durham. 300 plus members but unpredictable months, relying on talent not system. Three layer problem framework (external, internal, philosophical) stabilized close rate and made revenue forecastable.
13. Christine, Rebuilt Fitness. Consultations were feature dumps. Four pillar framework: Discovery, Empathy, The Plan, The Call to Action. 25 to 36 new members per month in 30 days, same lead volume.
14. Michael and Kim, Lakeland Athletic Club. Under leveraged floor. Structured seminar promotion, coached floor conversations, trackable re engagement. Seminars sold out weeks ahead; over 4,000 dollars in new revenue from floor conversations in 90 days.
